Thalapathy Vijay

Chandrasekaran Joseph Vijay is an Indian actor and politician. He is the president of the political party Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK). In the 2026 Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly election, his party secured 108 seats in the 234-member assembly, making TVK the single largest party and marking a seismic debut in Tamil Nadu politics.

Latest Political Developments (May 2026)

TVK fell 10 seats short of the 118 required for an outright majority, resulting in a hung assembly. Vijay has formally written to Governor Rajendra Vishwanath Arlekar staking claim to form the government, ending a 59-year dominance shared between the DMK and AIADMK. Coalition talks are currently underway. Kamal Haasan's MNM has voiced support, stating that not inviting Vijay to form the government would amount to "disrespecting the people's mandate."

Electoral Highlights

Vijay contested from two constituencies — Perambur (North Chennai) and Tiruchirappalli East — winning both. The April 23 polling recorded a historic 85.1% voter turnout, the highest ever for a Tamil Nadu assembly election. TVK also claimed approximately 35% of the popular vote, outpolling both Dravidian giants in their very first electoral attempt.

About Vijay

Born to film director S.A. Chandrasekhar and playback singer Shoba Chandrasekhar, Vijay made his acting debut as a child in Vetri (1984) and took his first lead role in Naalaiya Theerpu (1992). Known globally as "Thalapathy" (Commander), he is one of Tamil cinema's biggest superstars before transitioning to politics.
